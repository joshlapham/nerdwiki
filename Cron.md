## Basics

### List all crontabs -

`crontab -l`

### New crontab -

`crontab -e`

### Wipe all jobs from crontab -

`crontab -r`

## Format of crontab

`m h dom mon dow commandtorun`

1. Minute
2. Hour
3. Day of month
4. Month
5. Day of week

then command to run.

### Run job on reboot -

`@reboot echo hello`

### Run job every 5 minutes -

`*/5 * * * * echo hello`

## Cool crontabs

### Turn screensaver on every night @ 3am -

`0 3 * * * xset s on`

### Create backup of home folder every night @ 4:20am - 

`20 4 * * * tar -zcf /mnt/files/jlhomebak.tgz /home/jl/`
